My Favourite Hobby

Playing Badminton

My favourite hobby is playing badminton. I love playing badminton as it gives me joy, bliss and contentedness.I daily go and play badminton with my friends and I enjoy it very much. I have been playing this sport from the age of 4. My sister and my grandfather are the main people who have taught me to play this sport. My role model is P.V.Sindhu. Another thing that I like about badminton is that it is among the rare sports that men and women play together and people of all ages can play badminton. I really love playing badminton and I never get tired of playing this amazing sport.
